meet the artist.

Vincent Bercasio, an aspiring nineteen year old Hawaii based photographer, has been dominating the photography over Hawai’i with his unique editorial work and abstract composition. Bercasio has challenged the tra- ditional art of photography; his sense of depth and color scheme also playing a big part of his success. With each passing day, this upcoming photographer continuously grows in his work and would like to take his talent to the next level by showcasing his work to future clients and employers.
